About Me

Mar 18th, 2014

My name is Serj L and I’ve been ruby developer since 2012. I love Ruby and Ruby on Rails, because they provide such elegant ways to solve my problems and problems of my clients.

I have contributed to the Rails, Cancancan, Rails-18n and a lot of other gems. Also I helped ready4rails4 project (I did a lot of researches to get statuses for a lot of gems)!
